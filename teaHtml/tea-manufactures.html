Dilmah Tea Factory

The family tea business Dilmah of Sri Lanka has pioneered consistency, authenticity and diversity in tea, founded by Merrill J. Fernando, the world's most expert team builder. In 1988, when his family business defied market trends to announce its commitment to authenticity, Dilmah pioneered the idea of Single Origin Tea. Garden new, unblended tea is a characteristic of Dilmah and provides an unmixed, source packed ceylon tea.
Dilmah tea is Ethical tea since the MJF Charitable Foundation and Dilmah Conservation funds all the benefits of the packaging in Sri Lanka. Company is, we say, a human support business.

Ceciliyan Tea Factory

For over 150 years in Sri Lanka, ceylon tea cultivation has been a passion. During this time , global demand grew exponentially. The peaceful Sinharaja Rain Forest has a special environment with a biodiversity of surroundings. It crosses Cecilian land north and is a UNESCO UNESCO UNESCO World Heritage Site and Biosphere Reserve.
The Ceciliyan Estate is in complete harmony with nature, covering a total of 120 hectares in the green hills of Kalawana in the Ratnapura district. It was run by Ceciliyan Associates Private Ltd, a third-generation family company, in the early 1920s. Ceciliyan is known to be the original owner's wife's name and was licensed by the authorities after 1940.
